Pricing Policy

Introduction

At Burrpy, we are committed to providing our customers with high-quality, home-cooked meals at transparent and fair prices. This Pricing Policy outlines how we determine prices for our services, any additional fees, and our policy regarding price changes.

Base Pricing

Our meal prices are determined based on the cost of preparation charged by our partners. Prices for each meal are listed on our website and mobile app.

Delivery Fees

A delivery fee is applied to each order to cover the cost of delivering your meal directly to your doorstep. The delivery fee varies based on your location and will be displayed at checkout.

Subscription Plans

For regular customers, we offer subscription plans at discounted rates. Subscribers can choose from weekly or monthly plans, with the price depending on the number of meals selected.

Discounts and Promotions

From time to time, we may offer promotional discounts or special offers. These will be advertised on our website and through our email newsletters.

Taxes

All prices are inclusive of applicable sales taxes according to local laws.

Price Changes

We reserve the right to adjust our prices at any time. However, we will provide advance notice of any price changes through our website and email notifications.

Price Discrepancies

If there is a discrepancy between the price displayed online and the price charged, please contact us immediately for resolution.

Feedback

We value your feedback on our pricing. If you have any questions or concerns about our Pricing Policy, please contact us at contact@burrpy.com.
